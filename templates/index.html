Exoplanet Classifier

This is an exoplanet classifier. The exoplanet classes are based on those outlined by NASAs exoplanet catalog (https://exoplanets.nasa.gov/what-is-an-exoplanet/planet-types/overview/): Gas Giant, Neptune-like, Super Earth, and Terrestrial. The model predicts classes at 98.65% accuracy with just the planets mass, radius and density (g/cm**3) as inputs. The project repository is linked. Select your units, enter your values, and it will output one of the four classes below. Please note that density is a product of mass and radius so incorrect densities may result in an incorrect classification.
